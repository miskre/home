    faqs

    • What is
      is a decentralized currency that uses two native tokens MIS and KRE allowing users to receive and transfer payment in a fair and trustless system.
    • What problems aresolving?
      solves the centralization issue in blockchain governance by building a true democratic system of governance that uses ranked choice voting in a unique delegates system, along with an automated protocol proposal system.

      solves the ICO funding craze by building sensible incubators that would be able to invest in tens or even hundreds of startups with the amount of money raised in one successful ICO.

      solves accessibility in the space by empowering entrepreneurs all around the world to build on the miskre protocol, and bring their technology to local markets increasing miskre adoption worldwide.
    • How isdifferent from other protocols?
      is a true open source system that requires no maintainers, anyone can propose protocol updates that if gets voted in will automatically be deployed.incorporates many on-chain governance features that will prevent exploitation of the system.
    • What are MIS and KRE?
      The miskre ecosystem consist of two native tokens MIS and KRE.

      MIS represents management rights to the network, and by just simply holding it in a wallet it will generate KRE which is the currency of the network.
    • Will MIS generate KRE forever?
      No, to give early adopters incentive MIS will only generate KRE for approximately 22 years with the rate of KRE generated decreasing every year.
      • 16% of KRE will be generated first year
      • 52% of the KRE generated within the first four years
      • 80% KRE generated in twelve years
    • How many MIS and KRE will there be?
      There will be 20 billion MIS. 0 KRE at the start but will generate over the course of 22 years until the total reach 20 billion KRE.
